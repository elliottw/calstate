ELEC 202

Introduction to Higher Education for Engineers

Description
University structure, policies, and procedures, resources available, and skills necessary for success. Introduction to profession of engineering; engineering, design process; communication skills in engineering. Laboratory 3 hours. Graded CR/NC.
Learning Outcome
Upon successful completion of the course, each student should be able to understand and apply concepts and skills that will enhance their success and a student and as an engineer.
Text
Landis, Raymond B. (2007). Studying Engineering: A Road Map to a Rewarding Career (3rd Ed.) Los Angeles: Discovery Press.
